# Product Scope

Nexa WebApp delivers specialized modules designed to address distinct bounded contexts:

## 1. Sales Context
- **Commercial Orders**: Enables operators to validate client orders, verify pricing totals, and manage status transitions.
- **Buyer Portal**: Allows registered clients to browse the active catalog, add items to cart, submit purchase requests, and review order logs.
- **Clients Management**: Directory of B2B partners, company billing IDs, and contract details.

## 2. Warehouse Context
- **Inventory Lots**: Real-time tracking of cold-storage inventory items, including manufacturing dates, expiration dates, and lot numbers.
- **Warehouse Zones**: Graphical tracking of cold zones (e.g. Freezing Rooms vs. Chilled Rooms) to ensure proper temperature rules.

## 3. Logistics Context
- **Dispatch Planning**: Grid for dispatchers to schedule route assignments and vehicles.
- **Delivery Monitoring**: Route logs showing status updates and temperature readings throughout transport.

## 4. Invoicing Context
- **Business Documents**: Consolidated view of invoices, billing references, and PDF logs.
- **Payments Matching**: Registration log for matching commercial payments with pending invoices.
